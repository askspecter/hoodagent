Privacy Policy

Last updated 22 July 2026

HOLT is built to keep your data with you. This policy explains what the HOLT website at useholt.fun does and does not collect, and how the open-source HOLT command-line tool handles your information.

The short version. No account. No sign-up. No analytics or telemetry. Your sessions and settings stay in your browser on your device, and are never sent to us.

1.Information we do not collect

The HOLT website does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, or any personal details. We do not run analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or telemetry, and we do not build a profile of you.

2.Information stored on your device

To make the terminal usable, HOLT stores a small amount of data in your browser using localStorage, such as your theme, font size, and local session history. This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to us and we cannot read it. You can clear it at any time from your browser settings.

3.Credentials and keys

The website is a demo and does not ask for or use any real keys. When you run the open-source command-line tool, any API keys, tokens, or brokerage credentials you provide live in your own environment on your own machine. They are used only to talk directly to the services you choose, and are never sent to HOLT.

4.Third-party services

  • Hosting. The website is served by our hosting provider, which may process basic technical request data (such as IP address and user agent) in server logs to deliver and secure the site.
  • Fonts. The site loads a web font from Google Fonts, which may receive a request from your browser to serve it.
  • Services you connect. If you connect a brokerage such as Robinhood or an AI model provider through the command-line tool, your data flows directly between you and them under their own privacy policies. HOLT is not in the middle.

5.Cookies

The website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. It uses only local browser storage as described above, strictly to run the app on your device.

6.Children

HOLT is intended for adults who are legally able to trade in their jurisdiction. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of majority.

7.Data requests

Because we do not collect or hold your personal data, there is nothing for us to export or delete on your behalf. Data stored locally in your browser is fully under your control and can be cleared by you at any time.

8.Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always posted here with its effective date.

9.Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to us on X at x.com/useHOLT.
